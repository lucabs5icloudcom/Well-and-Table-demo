Our Story

A place to slow
down and gather.

Tucked inside the historic Gilman Village, The Well & Table grew out of a simple belief: the best meals happen when good ingredients meet good company. We source produce from farms across the Cascade foothills, work with local fishers, and bake everything in-house.

Our menu shifts with the seasons — not because it's fashionable, but because a tomato in July and a turnip in February deserve different treatment. We cook the food we want to eat, and we set a table we'd want to sit at.

The name nods to the village's own history: Gilman's old well, the original gathering point. We like the idea that a restaurant can still be that — a place you return to, not just for the food.
